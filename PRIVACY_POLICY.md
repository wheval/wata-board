# Privacy Policy - Wata Board

**Effective Date: April 25, 2026**

Wata Board ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Account Information**: Your user ID, public keys, and profile details.
* **Financial Data**: Transaction history, meter IDs, and payment amounts related to our services.
* **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage logs.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
* To provide and maintain our services.
* To process transactions and send related information.
* To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.
* To improve our application and user experience.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Transactional data is stored on the Stellar blockchain, which is public by nature. Off-chain data is stored in secure databases with encryption at rest.

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
* Service providers (e.g., RPC node providers, payment gateways).
* Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law.

## 5. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
* Access the personal data we hold about you.
* Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
* Object to processing of your data.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@wataboard.io.
